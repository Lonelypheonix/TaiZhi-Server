About Project

We are on the brink of total water scarcity and need more ways to save water. Volnix is a powerful water management system that can be used to save water. It is an IoT based device which measures soil moisture and based on the thresh hold set by the users it will water the plants. This is a step towards saving water and building a sustainable future.

It basically uses a sensor that senses the moisture content of the soil and when it is below the thresh hold level it automatically turns on the pump. Volnix can be monitored from any part of the world as it will be connected to the internet. This feature lets you monitor your crops remotely.

Most of the time we water plants irrespective of whether it is required or not. On summer days it might require more water and on cooler days it might require less water. The volnix makes sure this is done efficiently and water is not wasted. Not only that but also it can be implemented in the student's house so that he doesn't have to bother about watering the plants anymore.
